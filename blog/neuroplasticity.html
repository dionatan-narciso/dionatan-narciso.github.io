Neuroplasticity & Growth Mindset


Neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity is the practice of doing something mentally or physically and teaching that particular area of your brain to involve itself. In studies the practice of Neuroplasticity shows that the brain is like a muscle and it can be stretched and refined.
The most important is that we are all capable of learning anything, with a lot of mental and physical practice your brain can be adjust do the new challenges.

Growth Mindset

Growth Mindset is the ability to think that you are capable of doing something you do not understand yet, it is allowing your brain to think that you are able to achieve something with a lot of effort and a positive mindset, and with patience and time that will happen.
