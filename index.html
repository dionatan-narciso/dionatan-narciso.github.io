Travel Log

Amazing Places

The first place of my list is China, it is definitely one of the most diverse countries that I have been. From big and developed cities to places forgotten in ruins and forests. China is surprisingly a very organised country with an amazing public transport and despite having almost 1.5 billion people you will hardly see a crowd as everything works underground.

The second place is Hong Kong, one of the most crowded but yet fascinating cities in the world. Hong Kong is full of life at all times, everything is open 24h/7 and you will find very nice markets on the street with amazing “very cheap” stuff and diverse food.

Last but not least is my home country Brazil. There you will find the most accommodating people in the whole world and amazing places to go. Brazil has beautiful beaches with white sand and breath-taking forests and waterfalls. From south to north you will likely find yourself feeling like you are in a different country as Brazil has a huge diversity of culture including German and Italian immigrants on south to African and Portuguese on north. I would also recommend everyone to try the Brazilian cosine as it is unique and very local depending on where in the country you are.
